Hello World(s)!

I'm Andreas (Drey) Jonathan and i'm a Master of Sciecne in Library and Information Science candidate at Pratt institute School of Infromation, graduating February 2021.

I come to librarianship as a Black, Queer, Non-binary and first-generation American, by way of Ghana and Nigeria interested in critical librarianship practices, the politics of fairness and inclusion in algorithmic decision systems and artificial intelligence. I think a lot about how the ways we think about data and data-centric technologies influence how we approach them, regulate them and what kinds of relationships we build with and through them.
